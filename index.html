What We Do

Our policies made to help make branded apparel more accessible to small businesses.

No Minimum Order Size.

Most of our competitors in Edmonton demand a minimum order size that is impractical unless you have a huge budget. We don't force a minimum order size on our clients - you can order any amount.

Industry-first Warranty.

We stand behind the craftsmanship of our printed apparel and offer warranties on our prints to ensure your satisfaction.

Talk to Real People.

Every order you place will be assigned to a dedicated representative who'll help you make the best choices on your order from types of fabrics to designs.

Reprints.

Find a shirt you really like? We are one of the only print shops to offer printing on your garments. Provide us with the garment and we'll print your design on it.

Turnaround Times.

We have some of the fastest turnaround times in the game - we've regularly printed and delivered multi-item orders in 24 hours or less.

Unlimited Colours.

Our superior print technology allows us to offer your unlimited color prints at no extra cost, and being more true to the design specification.
